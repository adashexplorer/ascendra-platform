Usage

On this dark ground the ground-side of each ramp is the dark end: dark steps (700–900) make tinted fills, hovers and subtle borders; 500 is each role’s base; light steps (100–300) carry text on those tints. In your own pages reach for a ramp step before reaching for color-mix() (the component layer uses color-mix() internally for its hover tints).
